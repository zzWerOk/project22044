Personal note
from Nora & FamilyThis memorial website was created in loving memory of our Daddy and Papaw, James L. Fouss, P.E., PhD, age 87, who passed away peacefully on February 12, 2024 at the home of his son, in Kyle, Texas. James, known to most as Jim, was born on February 22, 1936 in the Village of Warsaw, Coshocton County, Ohio to the late Raymond L. and Hazel (Sergent) Fouss. In addition to his parents, he is preceded in death by his wife of over 61 years, Judith L. (Theiss) Fouss. He is survived by his sister Patti (Roger) Woodford of Beverly, Ohio; daughter JoAnna P. “Jody” (Larry) Weaver and son James M. (Angela) Fouss; 5 grandchildren, Matthew Fouss, Audrey Fouss, Erin Piper, Katy Bridges, and Meagan Suehs; and 6 great-grandchildren, Johnathan Fouss, Laney and Anya Piper, Presley and Willa Bridges, and LeAnn Suehs, all from Texas.
